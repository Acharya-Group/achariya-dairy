Your Complete Dairy Business Partner

Achariya Annapurna Dairy Mitra is part of Achariya Technologies Pvt. Ltd. We provide end-to-end support for rural dairy entrepreneurs — from buffalo financing to milk collection, product supply, and technical training.

Buffalo Finance & Support

We help dairy entrepreneurs access easy financing for high-yield buffaloes, along with veterinary care and insurance assistance. Our comprehensive buffalo support program ensures you get the best start in your dairy journey.

  • Easy EMI options for buffalo purchase with minimal documentation
  • Regular health checkups & vaccination support at your doorstep
  • Comprehensive insurance coverage for livestock protection
  • Best breed guidance for higher milk yield and better returns
  • 24/7 veterinary helpline for emergency assistance
  • Nutrition & fodder management consultation
