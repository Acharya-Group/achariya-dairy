About Achariya Annapurna Dairy Mitra

Helping Village People Start Their Own Dairy Business

Achariya Annapurna Dairy Mitra is part of Achariya Technologies Pvt. Ltd. We help young people and farmers in villages start their own dairy business. With our help, they can produce milk, collect milk, and sell dairy products — all in one place.

  • One place to produce, collect, and sell dairy products.
  • Many ways to earn money — Milk, Sweets, Spices (Masale), and Pickles (Aachar).
