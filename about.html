About Achariya Dairy Mitra

Empowering Rural Entrepreneurs Through Dairy

Achariya Dairy Mitra is a rural dairy entrepreneurship model by Achariya Technologies Pvt. Ltd. We empower rural youth and farmers to become self-reliant dairy entrepreneurs by integrating milk production, collection, and product sales under one hub.

  • Integrated hub for production, collection & retail.
  • Multiple income streams: Milk, Sweets, Masale, Aachar.
  • 6% p.a. interest with 6-month moratorium. Zero PMU commission.
