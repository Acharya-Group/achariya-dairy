Milk Collection in Every Village

02

Dairy Product Shops in Villages

We build a network of small shops in villages to sell milk, sweets, spices, and pickles easily.

03

Training for Dairy Business

We train farmers and young people on how to run a dairy business and give them a certificate to show their skills.

04

Support for Women and Young People

We give rural women and youth a chance to start their own business with full support and guidance from our team.

05

Digital Payment and Supply Chain

We connect the whole supply chain with mobile payments and digital tools — making it easy, fast, and clear for everyone.

06

Branding and Packaging Help

It is a simple village business. You buy buffaloes, produce milk, collect milk from your village, and run a small shop. Our company picks up the milk, makes products, and sends them back to you with branding. You earn money from selling milk, sweets, spices, and pickles.

We help you buy a good buffalo in a very easy way. You get the first 6 months free — no payments needed at the start. This gives you time to set up your business properly. And we charge zero extra commission.

You collect milk from your own buffaloes and from other people in your village. Then our company vehicle comes to your place, picks up all the milk, and takes it to our processing unit. You do not have to go anywhere — we come to you.

You can sell fresh milk, sweets, spices (masale), and pickles (aachar). All products are made at our unit and sent to you in nice branded packets. Everything is ready to sell — you just need to put it on your shelf.

We teach you everything — how to manage your dairy, collect milk, keep things clean, and sell products. After training you get a certificate. Our team also stays in touch to help you grow your business every single day.

Yes! You earn from milk, sweets, spices, and pickles — that is four ways to make money every month. With full support from Achariya Technologies and Modish Services, this is a steady business you can depend on for a long time.
