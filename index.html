About Achariya Dairy Mitra

Empowering Rural Entrepreneurs Through Dairy

Achariya Dairy Mitra is a rural dairy entrepreneurship model by Achariya Technologies Pvt. Ltd. We empower rural youth and farmers to become self-reliant dairy entrepreneurs by integrating milk production, collection, and product sales under one hub.

  • Multiple income streams: Milk, Sweets, Masale, Aachar.
  • 6% p.a. interest with 6-month moratorium. Zero PMU commission.

It's a rural entrepreneurship program where you become a Dairy Mitra agent. You produce milk (own buffaloes), collect from village, and run a retail hub. The company handles processing, branding, and logistics. You earn from milk, sweets, masale, and aachar sales.

We offer buffalo financing support at just 6% interest per annum. There's a 6-month moratorium period where interest is accrued but capitalized. Best of all, there is ZERO PMU commission.

As a Dairy Mitra agent, you collect milk from your own buffaloes and from other villagers in your hub. A company vehicle regularly picks up the collected milk and takes it to our central processing unit for further processing.

You can sell fresh milk, traditional sweets, masale (spices), and aachar (pickles). All products are processed at our central unit and sent back to you with full branding and packaging support from Modish Services Pvt. Ltd.

We provide complete training on dairy management, milk collection, hygiene practices, and sales. You'll receive certification and ongoing technical assistance to ensure your success.

Yes! With multiple income streams from milk, sweets, masale, and aachar, plus the backing of Achariya Technologies and Modish Services, this is a sustainable and scalable model designed for long-term rural self-employment.
